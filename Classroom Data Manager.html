Paste your Apps Script Web App URL (ends in /exec). Must be deployed with Execute as: Me and Who has access: Anyone.

Troubleshooting "Failed to fetch"
1
In Apps Script: Deploy → Manage deployments
2
Click the pencil/edit icon on your deployment
3
Under "Who has access" confirm it says Anyone — NOT "Anyone with a Google account"
4
If you changed anything, click Deploy — this creates a new URL. Update it here in Settings.
5
If still failing: try opening the /exec URL directly in a browser tab. It should return JSON, not an error page.
Goal ID matching

When you import from Embrace, each goal/objective is matched against your existing 91-goal bank using keyword overlap. You review and approve or override each match before saving. Students are stored with canonical G-IDs (G001, G039…) so they stay linked to your resources automatically.
